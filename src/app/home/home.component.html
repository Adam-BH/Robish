Beach Pollution Poses an Immediate Threat

Polluted beach water makes swimmers sick and hurts coastal economies. Illnesses associated with polluted beach water can range from stomach flu to hepatitis. In addition, there may be deep financial impacts: the economic loss for each day on which a beach is closed or under advisory for water quality problems can be quite significant. Not to mention its effects on marine life: bits of plastic swirl throughout the water column, even sinking to the deepest depths of the ocean, which then get consumed by many marine mammals.
